Privacy

Last updated: May 2026

Zero-Data Retention Policy

Your privacy is our core feature. When you upload an image to encode or decode a message, the image and its hidden contents are processed strictly in memory on our servers.

  • We do not save your uploaded original images.
  • We do not save your generated stego-images.
  • We do not log, store, or read your secret messages or passwords.

Analytics & Telemetry

The only data we store are anonymized metrics to power the public Analytics Dashboard. This includes: the file size, processing latency, algorithmic Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio (PSNR), and success/failure states. No personally identifiable information (PII) is ever collected.
